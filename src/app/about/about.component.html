Waste Segregations

Waste segregation is the sorting and separation of waste types to facilitate recycling and correct onward disposal. When waste is sorted correctly, it can save your company money. Waste segregation should be based on: The type of waste. The most appropriate treatment and disposal.

Setup Account

Step 1

Request a Tag with TagId/UUID from project developers.

Step 2

Use TagId to enroll account in app settings.

Step 3

Verified account with enrolled TagId should have check in app settings.

Step 4

Use Tag to scan account after disposal of waste in machine
